Live Weather Tools

Observe current atmospheric conditions with these real-time tools

Earth — Global Wind & Weather Visualization

A beautiful animated map of global weather conditions including wind, temperature, ocean currents, and more. Great for visualizing large-scale atmospheric circulation, jet streams, and storm systems in real time.

Open Earth ↗

Windy — Interactive Weather Forecast Map

A highly detailed interactive weather map showing wind, rain, temperature, cloud cover, and more. Supports multiple forecast models (ECMWF, GFS, NAM) and is widely used by pilots, sailors, and weather enthusiasts.

Open Windy ↗

Aviation Weather — GFA Prog Chart

The FAA / NOAA Graphical Forecasts for Aviation (GFA) tool. Displays current and forecast conditions across the contiguous US including clouds, icing, turbulence, and winds aloft — essential for flight planning and weather study.

Open GFA ↗
